Research Engagement

Our course integrates insights from recent research to enhance learning:

  • Exploring AI Definitions and Misconceptions - Discusses foundational AI concepts and common myths.
  • Data Science in Medical Applications - Focuses on preprocessing techniques in clinical data analysis.
  • Machine Learning Models - Details the application of machine learning models in healthcare.
  • Modern Methods in AI - Examines the role of neural networks in modern medical AI technologies.

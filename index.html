About Us

Empowering Medical Students with AI

In collaboration with UBC Data Science and Health, our workshop series offers a comprehensive introduction to the transformative power of artificial intelligence in medical practice. We aim to demystify AI for medical students, ensuring they are equipped to engage with this technology in their future clinical careers.

Our latest series was a tremendous success, reaching over 200 attendees in spring 2023. Explore our resources and find information on upcoming events below.

No prior technical background is required.

Course Overview and Resources

Comprehensive Tutorials

Participate in five 1-hour workshops covering core AI topics and their applications in medicine, such as machine learning techniques including kNN, random forests, and neural networks.

Guided Programming Projects

Hands-on project to set up and implement decision trees using Python, focusing on data processing skills critical for medical research and practice.

Evaluating AI Applications

Learn to critically assess AI technologies' capabilities and limitations in medical settings, enhancing your ability to make informed decisions in clinical contexts.

Engagement with AI Experts

Engage directly with leading AI researchers to discuss and understand real-world AI applications and their impact on modern medicine.
